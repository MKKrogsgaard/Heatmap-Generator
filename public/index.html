Heatmap generator

Drag and drop files or click to upload .fit file(s)

THE HEATMAP WILL APPEAR HERE
  • Upload one or multiple FIT files from activities with location data to generate a heatmap.
  • Your files are only stored temporarily while the heatmap is generated. Nothing is stored after you leave the site.
  • This site currently only supports FIT files (the format used by Garmin). I might add GPX support in the future.
  • If you don't have any FIT files, but still want to see how it works, click "GENERATE HEATMAP WITH EXAMPLE DATA" to generate a heatmap with data from some of my activities.
